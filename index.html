About Me


Hello, welcome to my portfolio! I'm John Miyahara, a Digital Design & Development student at Republic Polytechnic. In this website that I have programmed from scratch, you will be able to see my previous works as an aspiring audio and visual designer and developer.


Aside from academics, however, I also indulge in playing musical instruments as a member of Republic Polytechnic's acoustic band called Replug. Having been in the group for more than 2 years now, I have served as their Music Director for the year of AY2023/2024, and currently serve as their Student Advisor.


Do feel free to explore my projects and contact me at the 'Contact Me' page if you would like to get in touch!
